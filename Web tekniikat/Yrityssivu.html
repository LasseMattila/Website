COMPANY INTRODUCTION

ModCade is an arcade located in Miami, Florida. It was founded in 2005 and has grown to be the most successful arcade in the US. The name ModCade comes from the words modern arcade.

We have some of the oldest arcade machines there are, but we also have modern era simulator setups and virtual reality gaming setups.

We also have a few virtuix omnis in our premium rooms that can be rented by groups.

COMPANY ROOTS

The Company was started on a simple idea of joining people together in a nostalgic way to play all the old games from their childhood. The atmosphere was also a very important factor.

It had to have the same feeling that old arcades had. New technology was also necessary to offer enough variety for enjoyment in today’s world.

Not many people have a full-blown racing simulator sitting in their living room, so that aspect was easily covered.

INFO

ModCade employs around 20 servers/kitchen workers, 3 chefs, one accountant and one social media manager. We, the owners, manage other areas of the business.

We serve all kinds of snacks and finger foods, also some actual foods. Alcohol is also available, on weekend evenings/nights we have more of a bar vibe, but all the machines are also available at those times. We have about 70 different arcade machines, plus all the simulator setups. Some arcade machines we have several.
